About

Interior Design Built for
How You Actually Live

I'm Kristen, an interior designer and boy mom who specializes in creating family-friendly spaces that still feel grown-up and pulled together. I love designing homes where kids can actually live — climb, play, and make a mess — without turning every room into a "kid zone."

My focus is on low-lift projects for busy families who aren't ready for a full redesign but want to make small, impactful changes that won't break the bank. Whether it's rethinking a living room layout, refreshing a playroom, or layering in new textiles and storage, I work with what you already have whenever possible and recommend budget-conscious updates where they'll make the biggest difference.

My Approach

Design Philosophy

Three principles that shape every project I take on.

I

Kid-Friendly by Design

Beautiful spaces built for real family life. No off-limits rooms, no untouchable furniture. Every material and piece of furniture is chosen with the reality of children in mind.

II

Low-Lift, High Impact

Targeted changes that transform how a space feels without a full renovation commitment. I find the interventions that move the needle most — then focus there.

III

Real-Life Approved

Every recommendation considers durability, cleanability, and your actual budget. If I wouldn't put it in my own home with my boys, I won't recommend it to you.
